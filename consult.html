Support

Frequently Asked

How long does implementation typically take?
Standard builds take 1-2 weeks (3 weeks max) from audit to full deployment. More complex multi-agent systems involving proprietary data training may take 8-12 weeks. We move at the speed of your infrastructure.
Do we need to switch our existing tech stack?
No. We build "stack-agnostic" solutions. We plug into HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, GoHighLevel, and custom ERPs via secure APIs. Our goal is to enhance your current tools, not replace them.
Is my business data secure?
Security is our priority. We implement SOC2-compliant data handling. We use private, dedicated instances of LLM models to ensure your proprietary data never trains public models. Your intellectual property remains yours.
What is the expected ROI for AI automation?
Most clients achieve full payback within 4 months through payroll savings and increased capacity. Beyond cost, the ROI comes from speed and the ability to scale without linear hiring.
Can you automate my specific CRM?
If your CRM has an API, we can automate it. We have deep experience with HubSpot, Pipedrive, Zoho, and custom-built SQL-based systems. We specialize in cross-tool synchronization.
What kind of ongoing support do you offer?
We offer monthly "Optimization & Retain" packages where we fine-tune your prompts, update models as new tech releases (like GPT-5 or Claude 4), and monitor system logs to prevent errors.
